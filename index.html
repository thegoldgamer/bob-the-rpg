About

This is a bonus chapter of Bob - The RPG, made to help promote the Carthage Humane Society.

Controls

  • Use Z to progress through finished dialogue, and X to skip dialogue. Spamming Z and X will allow you to skip the dialogue that we worked hard on =(
  • Z is also to interact with things like doors, and people. Some doors you can just walk through, others you interact with. Other controls are explained in-game.
